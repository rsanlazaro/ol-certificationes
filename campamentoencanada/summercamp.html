Get to know us

For more than 15 years we have been offering a unique educational and cultural experience. The main objective of our language camp is to offer a learning environment, full of travel, surrounded by fun and education in a safe environment.

Program

Our educational program focuses on the development of communicative and written English through formal classes and informal social settings.

Trips

Weekend excursions include visits to Toronto, Quebec, Montreal, Niagara Falls and Ottawa and are designed to give participants a deeper understanding of Canada and its culture.

Why choose us?

Here are a few reasons why we are the right choice for you. We want you to have complete peace of mind and we want your children to have moments that will last a lifetime.

1

Venue

Our program takes place on the iconic campus of Carleton University in the unique city of Ottawa, Canada's capital.
2

Safety

Students are supervised by members of our specialized staff 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
3

Teachers

All of our English teachers are university graduates and are certified teachers who teach in Canadian schools during the school year.
4

Class time

We offer 5 hours of English instruction per day (maximum 15 students per teacher in the classroom).
5

Amenities

Students have access to the university's tennis courts, Olympic-sized swimming pool, gymnasiums and soccer fields, always supervised by trained personnel.
6

Memorable tours

Organized, educational and interesting weekend excursions to places such as: Toronto, Ottawa, Quebec, Montreal, Niagara, different museums and theme parks.
7

Cosmopolitan camp

Students from all over the world are enrolled, your children will be able to learn about other cultures and make international friends.
8

Evaluation

Results of a pre-screening test (upon arrival) and results of a final exam (upon departure). Formal graduation ceremony with the presentation of a certificate of participation.

What do we need to travel?

  • Fill out the camp registration form.
  • Copy of passport valid for at least 6 months.
  • Migration permit for minors and/or notarized letter.
  • Copy of updated birth certificate.
  • eTA Permit - What is an eTA? An Electronic Travel Authorization (eTA) is an entry requirement for visa-exempt foreign nationals traveling to Canada by air. Cost: $7 CAD $115 Mexican pesos.
